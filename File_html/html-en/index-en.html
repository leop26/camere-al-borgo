Welcome!

In the heart of Monteprandone, right in the beautiful Piazza dell’Aquila, our rooms welcome you with timeless charm. Fully renovated in 2024, they retain the warmth and elegance of antique, refined furnishings, creating a romantic and unforgettable stay.

Experience the Village

Being in the heart of Monteprandone means truly experiencing the village from the very first moment. Between cobbled streets and restored buildings, you’ll feel an authentic atmosphere, with romantic views and gentle hills dotted with vineyards. From the town square, everything is within reach: cafés, artisan shops, and hidden corners invite you to pause and enjoy village life, local flavors, and the scents of the Marche countryside.Life here flows slowly and pleasantly, perfect for walks, photos, and memorable moments.
