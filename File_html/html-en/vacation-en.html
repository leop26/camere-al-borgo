Discover Monteprandone
and surroundings

Monteprandone offers a variety of experiences between tradition, nature, and gastronomy. Historic streets, regional cuisine, gentle hills, and proximity to the Adriatic make the area perfect for a relaxing and diverse holiday.

Eating & Drinking

The region thrives on high-quality products: fresh fruits and vegetables, olive oil, wine, and the local cuisine. Many of these products can also be found in small local shops.

You can experience Marche cuisine and more in the many restaurants and bars nearby.
For those who prefer to prepare their own meals, a supermarket within walking distance provides all the essentials.

Historical Attractions

Even on a short walk, you can discover a lot in Monteprandone. The historic center is characterized by narrow streets, old stone houses, and small squares.

A central point is the Church of San Nicolò di Bari. Many other churches are scattered throughout the old town, waiting to be discovered. Monteprandone is also the birthplace of Saint Giacomo della Marca, whose story comes alive at the Civic Museum and the Santuario Santa Maria delle Grazie.

Nature & Activities

Around Monteprandone, hills and olive groves invite you to walks, hikes, or bike rides. The panoramic terraces offer stunning views all the way to the Adriatic coast.

For longer trips, the Cammino Francesco della Marca or the Monti Sibillini National Park (about 1 hour away) are great options.

The beaches of San Benedetto del Tronto are quickly reachable (10 minutes by car) and, like neighboring beaches, provide cooling on hot summer days.

Visitor Info

For more information on events and excursions, visit the tourist office of Monteprandone, located directly across from us.
